About Bonsai World

Bonsai World was found by five members Aadesh Shrestha, Gaurav Kumar Mahato, Utsab Katuwal, Krish Bhattarai and Sumit Pandit. It was established in 2022. It's a Startup Company which is about selling Bonsai Plants . Bonsai World is located in nepal and it's goal is to sell Bonsai to whole wordlwide



Bonsai at your Doorstep

Bonsai should be easy to get. Our goal is to make the world greener and make gardening easy for everyone.Each bonsai comes with easy to follow care instructions and our friendly plant doctors are on hand to hand if anything goes wrong.



Quality products

We buy directly from reputable growers that have earned the A1 quality seal so that we can offer the finest bonsai at the most competitive pricing.We carefully cultivate and inspect each bonsai to ensure that only the best ones reach it to your door. Tell us right away if you're not completely satisfied, and we'll return your money.

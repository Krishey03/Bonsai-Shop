Azalea Bonsai


Azalea is a very beautiful bonsai tree due to its flowers. They are an evergreen bonsai so even if the Azalea is not in bloom they are just as beautiful. Azalea are a gorgeous indoor bonsai species that produce beautiful flowers when blooming. Azaleas are available in different flower colors and bloom in late spring. They typically grow smaller leaves than most bonsai trees which makes them a perfect choice for a bonsai.



    Before you buy

  • Soil Type: Needs Moist Soil
  • Placement: Partially Shady Location
  • Fertilizing: Acidic Fertilizer
